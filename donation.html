SERVEY ON WATER SCARCITY

INTERACTION WITH LOCALITY PEOPLE


"In our survey, we observed that these are the problems faced by the people"

  1. Limited Access to Clean Water: Many individuals struggle to access safe drinking water due to infrastructure issues or geographical location.
  2. Health Issues: Contaminated water sources can lead to health problems, including waterborne diseases.
  3. Agricultural Impact: Farmers may experience reduced crop yields due to insufficient water, threatening food security
  4. Drought Conditions: Extended periods of drought can lead to severe water shortages for both personal use and agriculture.
  5. Pollution of Water Sources: Industrial waste and agricultural runoff can contaminate local water supplies.
