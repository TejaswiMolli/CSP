Welcome to Water Scarcity action force

Together for Sustainable Water Solutions

Our Mission

To raise awareness about water scarcity and promote the sustainable management of freshwater resources worldwide.

Water Scarcity: A Growing Challenge

Water scarcity affects billions of people globally. Whether due to climate change, over-extraction, or pollution, many communities struggle to access clean water for their daily needs. Here, we explore the causes and consequences of water scarcity, highlighting the urgent need for action.

Field Projects

Construction of ditches and canals in Sudan Village in central Sudan to provide residents with drinking water.

River dredging in Wang Nam Khiao Town in Thailand for safe sailing and riverine system maintenance.

Trough construction in Sistan province in Iran for endangered species.

Research Projects

Algae: The Baltic Sea Monster. This project aimed to discover the causes, effects and solutions for Baltic Sea pollution.

Oil spill detection, This project aimed to detect oil spills in Persian Gulf by using satellite Radar images.

Assessment of the possibilities of tide mills in Atlantic Ocean to produce clean energy.
